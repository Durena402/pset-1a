Carnival Pride Ship Itinerary!

  1. First Day: Board Ship at Baltimoe Port!

    2. It's time to start you vacation right by joining the party on the main deck where the DJ will be playing music all day long! Its the party you can't miss with everyone dancing and having a good time.

  2. Second Day: First Day at Sea!

    3. After all the fun from yesterday it's time to get to know your ship and explore all the cool spots to hang out around the ship like the water slides and basketball court for the kids or the casino and theatre to view several perfomrances that take place throughout the day. Also enjoy the all day buffet that has a variety of options to choose from.

  3. Third Day: Day at Sea!

    4. Now it's time to relax and enjoy the salt water pool or lay in the sun on all the chairs set up on the outside decks and enjoy the peace and quiet while staring out into sea and just get lost in it all.

  4. Fourth Day: Arrive at The Grand Turks!

    5. You have finally reached land and it is time to explore one of the many islands apart of The Grand Turks! This island has beautiful beaches and many excursions such as snorkeling and more! If you would like to see a video of the island presss here.

  5. Fifth Day: Arrive at Princess Cays!

    6. On this day you will visit the island Princess Cays where you can spend the whole day in the beautiful beach swimming in the clear water or just relaxing on the beach chairs. There are also a few excursions that you can take part in such as touching sting rays and more! If you would like to see a video of the island presss here.

  6. Sixth Day: Arrive at FreePort!

    7. On this day you will arrive at your final stop before heading back home. You can take tours of the island and visit their beautiful beaches. Although you only spend a little time on this island, you can still have a lot of fun. There are also excursions that you can join to make your experience better!If you would like to see a video of the island presss here.

  7. Seventh Day: Day at Sea!

    8. Today is your last full day aboard this ship as we are sailing back to the port of Baltimore! Hope you have an amazing day!

  8. Eight Day: Arrive to Baltimore!

    9. You vacation has finally come to end sadly, but we hope you had a great experiencing while aboard Carnival Pride. We hope to see you back soon!
